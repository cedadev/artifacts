Conventions

  • "Year" means financial year (April to March).
  • Quarter numbers correspond to the following time periods.
    1April-May-June
    2July-August-September
    3October-November-December
    4January-February-March
  • Targets and milestones printed in blue and marked with an asterisk appear in the NERC-BADC SLA Annex of the corresponding financial year.

  • The current cost of data archival is estimated to be about £2K per Tb. This includes disk space or tape storage, physical maintenance and backups. For early programmes and for programmes which are not likely to involve a considerable volume of data, this cost is supported by BADC core funding.
