Past BADC Supplementary Activities

BADC supplementary activities have included

• The data management of NERC Thematic and Directed Mode Programmes:

  • ACSOE - Atmospheric Chemistry Studies in the Oceanic Environment, 1995-2000
  • ARDAAOS - Assimilation of Remote-sensed Data for Appliactions in the Atmospheric and Oceanographic Sciences, 1998-2003
  • URGENT - Urban Regeneration and the Environment, 1996-2003
  • COAPEC - Coupled Ocean-Atmosphere Processes and European Climate, 2000-2005

• Other activities:

  • Data management for the Data Assimilation Research Centre (DARC), 2001-2003 - Now included in BADC Core Activities
  • Update of the NERC Metadata Gateway, 2001-2004
  • Data management for the HiRDLS instrument, 2003-2004
