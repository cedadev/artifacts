QUEST Theme 1: The contemporary carbon cycle and its interactions with climate and atmospheric chemistry

QUEST aims to improve our understanding of the feedbacks among physical, chemical, and biological processes that have contributed to determining the atmospheric greenhouse gas content today and its possible evolution over the 21st century.

Projects that have been funded in this theme:

  • QUAAC - Quest Atmospheric Aerosols and Chemistry
  • QUERCC - Quantifying ecosystem roles in the carbon cycle
  • MarQUEST - Marine Biogeochemistry and Initiative in QUEST
  • CCMAP - Climate-carbon modelling, assimilation and prediction
  • Feedbacks QUEST - Quantifying the influence of biogeochemical feedbacks on climate change
