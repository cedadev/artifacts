Access to AMMA forecasts from Reading University is restricted to AMMA participants who have electronically signed the NERC - UK Met Office Agreement as part of the online dataset application. To apply for access, please follow the procedure described in the Access to Data section of the AMMA Welcome Page.

To access the latest forecast products from the University of Reading, please see section below. To access past forecast products from the University of Reading, please browse the AMMA forecasts archive.
