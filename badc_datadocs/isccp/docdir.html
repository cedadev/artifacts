ISCCP Document Directory

The cd-rom document directory holds the contents of the printed booklet which accompanies the CD-ROM and three descriptive catalogs for the ISCCP Stage C1 and C2 data, the ISCCP Snow/Ice Products, and the ISCCP TOVS data as standard ASCII files (go to the indexed documentation for more details).

However, please note that the only data on this disk are the ISCCP-C2 data, which are the monthly statistics, at both three-hour-monthly and monthly temporal resolutions.
The ISCCP-B3, ISCCP-TOVS, ISCCP-Ice/Snow, and ISCCP-C1 data are available to NASA-funded researchers through NASA's Climate Data System, now part of Goddard's Distributed Active Archive Center (DAAC) or through the official ISCCP Central Archive:
