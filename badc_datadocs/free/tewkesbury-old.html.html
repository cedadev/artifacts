FREE - Aerial images of Tewkesbury flooding

Introduction

Aerial images of the Tewkesbury area were collected during the flood events of summer 2007. These images have been archived at the BADC.

This work was funded by NERC - through the Flood Risk for Etreme Events (FREE) NERC directed mode programme.

Access to data and information

The images will be stored at the BADC in the FREE Tewkesbury archive. Metadata will also be stored at the BADC .

Access to FREE data held at the BADC will be restricted to the FREE participants during a restricted access period ending two years after the end date of each project. Data will be made publicly accessible after that date.

If you are a FREE participant and wish to access restricted FREE data, please

  • register as a BADC user — if you are already a registered BADC user, skip this step; if you have forgotten your BADC user ID and/or password, please contact the BADC helpdesk at badc@rl.ac.uk;
  • apply for access to the free archive — you will be asked to abide by the FREE Data Protocol clauses.
