Mauna Loa

Mauna Loa, USA   (NDSC Hawaii Station)
Latitude:    19.54    
Longitude: -155.58

Ozone Lidar

I. S. McDermid, T. D. Walsh, and E. W. Sirko (JPL) - Multi-wavelength system (ozone, T, and aerosol); deployed in July 1993.

Trop. Ozone Lidar

M. H. Proffitt (NOAA/CIRES) - Being tested at Fritz Peak (39°N, 105°W) for possible 1997 deployment.

T and Aerosol Lidar

I. S. McDermid, T. D. Walsh, and E. W. Sirko (JPL) - Multi-wavelength system (ozone, T, and aerosol); deployed in July 1993.

T and Aerosol Lidar

J. E. Barnes (NOAA) - Ruby and Nd:YAG systems; aerosol database extends back to 1974 for ruby system and April 1994 for Nd:YAG system; temperature retrievals began in June 1994.

UV/Vis. Spectr.

P. V. Johnston (NIWA) and B. A. Bodhaine (NOAA) - NIWA system deployed in July 1996 for NO2 and ozone; will be upgraded for BrO and OClO measurements in 1997.

Microwave Ozone

A. Parrish (Millitech & U. MA) and J. J. Tsou (GATS Inc.) and B.J. Connor (NIWA)- One of two Millitech-built systems; installed in 1995 following testing and intercomparisons at Table Mountain Facility (34.4°N, 117.7°W). Participated in intercomparison at Mauna Loa in July 1995. Retrieving profiles from 20 to 64 km from July 1995 to April 1996 and continuing from August 1996.

Microwave H2O

R. Bevilacqua, P. R. Schwartz, and G. Nedoluha (NRL) - NRL Water Vapor Microwave Spectrometer #3 (WVMS3) operating since March 1996 following testing at Table Mountain Facility (34.4°N, 117.7°W).

FTIR

D. Murcray and F. Murcray (U. Denver) - Bomem spectrometer operated from 1991 to 1995; automated Bruker system installed August 1995.

Ozonesondes

S. Oltmans (NOAA) - Weekly soundings from Hilo, HI.

Spectral UV

R. L. McKenzie and P. V. Johnston (NIWA) and B. A. Bodhaine (NOAA) - Measurements of the spectral distribution of UV radiation with a double monochromator initiated in 1995.
