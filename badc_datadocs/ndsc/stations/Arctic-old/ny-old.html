Ny Aalesund

Ny Aalesund, Norway   (NDSC Arctic Station)
Latitude:    78.92    
Longitude:   11.93
WWW: http://www.npolar.no/nyaa-lsf/nyaa-info.htm

Ozone Lidar

R. Neuber and O. Schrems (AWI) - Multi-wavelength system (excimer and Nd:YAG) making winter measurements between 13 and 45 km since 1991.

T and Aerosol Lidar

O. Schrems and R. Neuber (AWI) - Multi-wavelength system (excimer and Nd:YAG) making winter measurements between 10 and 45 km since 1991.

UV/Vis. Spectr.

G. Braathen (NILU) - SAOZ NO2 and ozone system operating since fall 1990; new NILU system for measuring OClO and BrO as well as NO2 and ozone installed August 1995.

Microwave Ozone

K. Kuenzi (U. Bremen) - Campaign operations in winters of 1992/93 and 1993/94; permanent operation since November 1994.

Microwave ClO

K. Kuenzi (U. Bremen) - Campaign operations in winter of 1993/94; permanent operation since January 1995.

FTIR

O. Schrems and J. Notholt (AWI) - Bruker 120-HR with 0.0025-cm-1 resolution making solar and lunar (polar night) observations since May 1996. Previous measurements taken with a Bruker 120-M, which is now used as a mobile instrument (see entry under Mobile Instruments section).

Ozonesondes

H. Gernandt and P. von der Gathen (AWI) - Year-round soundings since 1992.
