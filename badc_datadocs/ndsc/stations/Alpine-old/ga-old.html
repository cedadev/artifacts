Garmisch

Garmisch, Germany   (NDSC Alpine Station)
Latitude:  47.48    
Longitude: 11.06

Aerosol Lidar

H. Jaeger, and F. Homburg (IFU) - System similar to the instrument at Mauna Loa (19.54°N, 155.58°W) operated as a ruby system from 1976 to 1990 and as a Nd:YAG system in operation since 1991.

Spectral UV

G. Seckmeyer (Fraunhofer Institute) - Measurements of the spectral UV irradiance since 1993 in Garmisch and since 1995 at Zugspitze.
