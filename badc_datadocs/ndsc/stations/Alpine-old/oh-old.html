Observatoire de Haute Provence

Observatoire de Haute Provence, France   (NDSC Alpine Station)
Latitude:  43.94    
Longitude:  5.71

Ozone Lidar

S. Godin (CNRS) - Began NDSC operations in January 1991. Database extends back to October 1986. New lidar system installed in 1994.

Trop. Ozone Lidar

G. Ancellet (CNRS) - Tropospheric ozone measurements; database extends back to 1986.

Aerosol Lidar

C. David and Ph. Keckhut (CNRS) - Began NDSC operations in January 1991. Database extends back to 1986.

T Lidar

A. Hauchecorne and Ph. Keckhut (CNRS) - Began NDSC operations in January 1991. Database extends back to 1978.

UV/Vis. Spectr.

F. Goutail and A. Sarkissian (CNRS) - SAOZ system for NO2 and ozone; intercompared with NOAA and New Zealand systems in 1992.

Ozonesondes

C. Vialle (IPSL) and F. Goutail (CNRS) - Database extends back to 1986.
