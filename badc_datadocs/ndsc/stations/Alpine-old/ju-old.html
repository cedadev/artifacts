Jungfraujoch

Jungfraujoch, Switzerland   (NDSC Alpine Station)
Latitude:  46.50    
Longitude:  8.00

UV/Vis. Spectr.

P. C. Simon (IASB) - SAOZ system being operated as a component of the ESMOS Alpine Station since mid-1990.

Microwave Ozone

N. Kaempfer and R. Peter (U. Bern) - Profile measurements (15 to 70 km) at Bern (46.95°N, 7.45°E), 60 km from the Jungfraujoch, since November 1994.

Microwave ClO

N. Kaempfer and R. Peter (U. Bern) - Operated January 1992 through August 1994. Operations resumed in late 1996.

FTIR

R. Zander and P. Demoulin(U. Liège) - Double-pass grating instrument used until October 1989; two FTIR systems used since 1984 (0.0025 cm-1) and 1990 (0.001 cm-1). Database extends back to 1977 (limited data to 1950).
