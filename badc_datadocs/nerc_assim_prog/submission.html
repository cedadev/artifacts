Assimilation of Remote-sensed Data for Applications in the Atmospheric and Oceanographic Sciences (ARDAAOS) - Instructions to Data Providers

  • Preliminaries

    To upload your data files on the BADC computer, you will need
    1. to have registered as a BADC user and got a password;
    2. to have been granted access to the ARDAAOS archive.
    If you do not yet have access to the ARDAAOS archive set up at the BADC, then please apply for access to the ARDAAOS data.

  • Uploading data

    Data providers can upload their data files directly from their home computer into the BADC incoming directory, via the BADC Web based file uploader. This device can also be used to submit software and text files containing metadata or any relevant information on the project deliverables. Please have your BADC user ID and password handy. You will be asked to select a BADC subdirectory corresponding to your project(s). Subdirectory names are as follows.

    / incoming / nerc-assim-prog /... Project title
    altiocean The impact of in situ temperature and salinity data, and improved mean SSH fields on the assimilation of altimeter data in ocean models
    assioccam
    (1)Assimilation of altimeter, SST and in situ hydrographic data into the OCCAM global ocean model
    (2)Assimilation of altimeter, radiometer and in situ data into the OCCAM model. Analysis of water properties and transport
    ecosyscol Assimilating satellite ocean colour observations into oceanic ecosystem models
    oceancirc Application of an adaptive data-assimilation scheme to estimate eddy-scale near-surface ocean circulation from ERS sea surface temperature and height observations
    rainfalls High temporal resolution, regional-scale rainfall data: satellite retrieval, modelling and assimilation
    tropostra
    (1)Assimilation of satellite data using a general circulation model of the troposphere and stratosphere
    (2)Assimilation of research satellite data using a general circulation model of the troposphere and stratosphere
