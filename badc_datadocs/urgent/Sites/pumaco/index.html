URGENT: PUMA Sites

Sites in and around Birmingham where measurements have been made during the PUMA campaigns of Summer 1999 and Winter 2000.
At the same time as the PUMA campaigns were carried out, local observations were also made in the framework of the Automatic Urban Network (AUN), which is a government programme. Samples of these data are archived together with results from the PUMA campaigns. Some data were also provided by local authorities (LA).
Note that two aircraft measurement campaigns were conducted at the same time as the PUMA campaigns above the West Midlands, in the framework of URGENT Project No GST/02/2225.

A "clickable" map of PUMA sites will allow you to view the sites distribution in and around the West Midlands County.
