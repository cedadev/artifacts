URGENT Air Science Projects

This page provides information on the deliverables and archived data of the 13 air projects within the URGENT Thematic Programme. Air projects abstracts are also available from the URGENT Central Web Site. Click here for a summary of the types of scientific research conducted by all projects.

  • GST/02/1971 - A Thermal Climatology of the West Midlands
  • GST/02/1974 - Tracers and Dispersion of Gaseous Pollutants
  • GST/02/1981 - Observation, Modelling and Management of Urban Air Pollution (PUMA Consortium)
  • GST/02/1983 - Characterisation, Source-Receptor Modelling and Fate of Organics in Airborne Particles
  • GST/02/1996 - Development of a User-Friendly Software Package for Predicting the Concentration of Pollutant in the Atmosphere from Chimney Emissions in Urban Environments
  • GST/02/2090 - Diode Laser Detection of Peroxy Radicals in the Atmosphere
  • GST/02/2222 - Airborne Particulate Pollutants: Physicochemistry and Toxicity
  • GST/02/2225 - An Instrumented Aircraft Facility to Provide Vertical Profiles of Wind, Temperature, Turbulence, Sensible Heat, Aerosol and Trace-Gas Concentrations and Fluxes within the Urban Boundary Layer for PUMA Consortia Model Validation
  • GST/02/2229 - Development of a Lumped Gas Phase Mechanism for Use in Urban Chemical Transport Models
  • GST/02/2231 - Universities Weather Research Network (UWERN) Urban Meteorology Programme
  • GST/02/2244 - Sources and Sinks of Urban Aerosols
  • GST/02/2254 - Evolution of the Particle Size Distribution of Vehicular Emissions in the Urban Atmosphere
  • GST/02/2602 - Experimental Quantification and Modelling of Dispersion of Particles in Urban Street Canyons
