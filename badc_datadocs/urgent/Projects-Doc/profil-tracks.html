URGENT: GST/02/2225 Flight Tracks

Flight tracks of the CESSNA aircraft during the Summer 1999 and Winter 2000 campaigns of URGENT Project No GST/02/2225, "An Instrumented Aircraft Facility for Measurements of Trace Gas & Aerosol".
Flights 6 of the Summer 1999 campaign and 1 of the Winter 2000 campaign aborted due to unfavourable meteorological conditions. Each observation flight of the Winter 2000 campaign was preceded by a flight to Tattenhill airport to refuel.
