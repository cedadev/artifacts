URGENT: PUMA Data Summary
June-July 1999

Summary of data collected by Project No GST/02/1981 during the Summer 1999 campaign.
Some data were collected from a parked mobile unit. These are marked stat.
When collected while driving, data are marked move.
Chemical species measured from the pollution monitoring van parked at Pritchatts Road are marked van.
Meteorological and chemical data measured from the top of the ten-metre high tower are marked 10 m.
Data measured from the 5 m level of the tower are marked 5 m.
Ion measurements obtained with the micro-orifice uniform deposit impacter are marked MOUDI.
Ion measurements obtained with the Partisol Sampler are marked Partisol.
NO2* indicates that NO2 is calculated as [NO2] = [NOX] - [NO].
An exhaustive list of hydrocarbons is accessible by clicking on Hydrocarbons in the table below.
Longitude and latitude coordinates of the sites are given in the PUMA Sites List. To view the sites distribution, please look at the PUMA Sites Map.
