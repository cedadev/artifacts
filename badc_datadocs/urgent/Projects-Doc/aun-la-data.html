URGENT: AUN and LA Data Summary

Summary of data collected by the Automatic Urban Network (AUN) or provided by Local Authorities (LA), compiled in the framework of Project No GST/02/1981.
During the two PUMA campaigns of the Summer 1999 and Winter 2000, the Automatic Urban Network (AUN) was in operation. Relevant samples of the resulting data set are archived at the BADC. Data were also provided by local authorities (LA), covering the same periods of time.
Longitude and latitude coordinates of the sites are given in the PUMA Sites List. To view the sites distribution, please look at the PUMA Sites Map.
