Status of the ECMWF Operational Archive at the BADC

This page provides information about changes made to the BADC archive of ECMWF ERA-15 data.

The BADC holds ECMWF ERA-15 Analysis and Forecast data for the period January 1979 - February 1994.

Table 1 lists changes that have been made to archive that may be of interest to users.

Table 1.   Status of ECMWF ERA-15 data.
DATE STATUS CHANGE
19/01/2003 BADC replaces initialised data archive that was held on tape with a local disk version for faster data access.
