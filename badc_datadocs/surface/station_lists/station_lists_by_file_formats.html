Met Office Land Surface Station Lists by file formats type


  • Stations containing dwx files, i.e. measuring Daily weather parameters (sunshine amount, state of concrete slab, snow lying on the ground, snow depth, fresh snow amount, day of snow, hail, thunder and gale).
  • Stations containing temp files , i.e. measuring daily temperature extreme (maximum and minimun air temperatures, minimum grass temperature and minimum concrete temperature).
  • Stations containing drain files , i.e. measuring daily rain parameters (precipitation amount only).
  • Stations containing tsoil files , i.e.measuring daily soil temperature (between depths from 5 to 100cm).
  • Stations containing hwx files , i.e. measuring hourly weather parameters (10 minute wind speed and direction, present and past weather, cloud amount, type and base, visibility, MSL pressure, dry-bulb temperature, wet-bulb temperature, dew-point temperature, state of ground and 10 minute maximum gust speed).
  • Stations containing radt files , i.e. measuring hourly and daily radiation parameters (global, diffuse and direct solar radiation).
  • Stations containing hsun files , i.e. measuring hourly sunshine.
  • Stations containing wind files , i.e. measuring hourly mean wind parameters (mean wind direction and speed, max gust direction and speed and time of maximum gust).
  • Stations containing hrain files , i.e. measuring hourly rain (precipitation amount and duration).
