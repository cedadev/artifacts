References for the Met Office TOVS Analyses

Some pointers to additional information in the scientific literature about the Met Office TOVS Analyses

The Analysis System

The Principal References for the Met Office assimilation scheme are:

  • Bailey, M.J., A. O'Neill, V.D. Pope: Stratospheric Analyses produced by the UK Meteorological Office. J. Appl. Meteor. 32, pp 1471-1483 (1993)
  • Bailey, M.J., A. O'Neill, V.D. Pope: Stratospheric Analyses produced by the UK Meteorological Office. Climate Research Technical Note, No. 24, UK Met. Office. (1992)

  • Nash, J., J.L. Brownscombe: Validation of the stratospheric sounding unit. Adv. Space Res. 2, pp. 59-62 (1983)
  • Nash, J., J.L. Brownscombe, G.F. Forrester: Long-term monitoring of stratospheric temperature trends using radiance measurements obtained by the TIROS-N series of NOAA spacecraft. Adv. Space Res. 6, pp. 37-44. (1986)
