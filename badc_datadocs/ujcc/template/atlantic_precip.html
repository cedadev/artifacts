North Atlantic Precipitatoin

This movie shows the evolution of weather systems in the North Atlantic Storm Track. The images show the total precipitation rate (mm/hour) over the final month of the current UJCC (HiGEM) simulation. The series of images demonstrates the ability of the UJCC (HiGEM) model to capture the evolution (birth, growth and decay) of weather systems in the North Atlantic storm track. Movie image interval is 1 day.
