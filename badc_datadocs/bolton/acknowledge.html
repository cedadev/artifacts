How to Acknowledge Data from the Bolton Experiment

Data is available for research purposes only. If you acquire this data the following organisations should be acknowledged:

  • For the raingauge data, the providers - United Utilities and/or the Environment Agency- should be acknowledged. See site list for details.
  • For the weather-station and radar data, the UK Met Office should be acknowledged.
  • For the microwave-link data, reference should be made to :
    The use of dual-frequency microwave links for measuring path-averaged rainfall.
    A. R. Rahimi, A. R. Holt, G. J. G. Upton, and R. J. Cummings.
    Journal of Geophysical Research: Atmospheres, 108(D15), 4467, 2003.
