SAM II References

Some pointers to additional information about the SAM II data record.

The SAM II User's Guide

The principal reference for users wishing to work with the SAM II data is:

Chu, W. P., M. T. Osborn, and L. R. McMaster, SAM II Data Users' Guide, NASA RP-1200, July 1988.
