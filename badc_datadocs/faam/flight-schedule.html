FAAM Flight Schedule

Last update: 29 September 2005
Detailed information on the conditions of each past flight can be found in the FAAM Aircraft Usage Chart provided on the FAAM Web Site under "Aircraft - Schedule", and in the flight logs archived in the FAAM data archive. For data access conditions and application, please see the list of FAAM Campaigns. A click on the table header (Year XXXX) will lead you to the dataset corresponding to that year.
In the tables below, light grey cells signal numbered or unnumbered flights that were cancelled or during which no data were collected (certification, crew training, aircraft positioning, transit, photography, etc.).
