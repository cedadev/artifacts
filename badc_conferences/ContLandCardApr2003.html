Workshop on Contaminated Land

The Glamorgan Building, Cardiff University

3rd April 2003
10.30am - 4.00pm

An open workshop for URGENT scientists and end users to discuss research results, applications and the way forward.
Featuring:
  • Metal fluxes in contaminated land.
  • Mobilisation and transport of metals between ecosystem compartments in the Lower Swansea Valley.
  • Integrated assessment of modelling of soil contaminant behaviour, transport and impact at remediable urban sites.
  • Urban regeneration of coalfields.
